Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Learn English: Word Day ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the app:

  • Learning progress and statistics
  • Vocabulary lists and words you add
  • App settings and preferences
  • Answers and quiz results

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you use the app:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics and analytics
  • Crash reports and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and improve the service: To deliver educational content and personalized learning experience
  • Track your progress: To save your learning achievements and vocabulary
  • Improve the app: To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs
  • Personalization: To customize your learning experience based on your level and preferences

3. Data Storage

All your learning data is stored locally on your device. We use local storage mechanisms to ensure your data remains private and accessible offline. Your vocabulary lists, progress, and settings are not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly use cloud backup features (if available).

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services (for Android users)
  • Analytics services to understand app usage

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The app does not require users to create accounts or provide personal information. All data is stored locally on the device.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the app from unauthorized access and vulnerabilities.

However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app's settings
  • Deletion: You can delete your learning progress and vocabulary at any time through the app settings
  • Portability: You can export your data if the export feature is available
  • Correction: You can modify or update your learning data at any time

8. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your learning progress and vocabulary locally
  • Notifications: To send you learning reminders (only if you enable them)
  • Internet: To load educational content and articles (if available)

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

9. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We may use analytics tools to understand how users interact with the app and to identify and fix crashes. This information is anonymous and does not identify you personally.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
